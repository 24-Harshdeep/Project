How can I prepare for Natural Disasters?
You can prepare by creating an emergency kit with water, food, a flashlight, and a first-aid kit. Have an evacuation plan ready for your family.
What should I include in a first aid kit?
A first-aid kit should include bandages, antiseptic wipes, adhesive tape, tweezers, pain relievers, and a thermometer.
What is the best way to stay informed during an emergency?
Stay informed by tuning in to local news, having a weather radio, and signing up for emergency alerts on your phone.
